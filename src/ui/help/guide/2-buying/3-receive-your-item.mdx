## Receive your item

### Check your item

Once your item has arrived, check that everything is okay. Make sure it is in the advertised condition and that it actually works.

### Update your order

Head to the <AnchorButton target="_blank" href="/my/orders" className="inline-flex" kind="tertiary">My Orders</AnchorButton> page to let us know your order has arrived.

<img src={guide16} className="max-w-screen-xs !my-12" />

> It's important to let us know when your item has arrived so we can process the order and transfer your funds to the seller

<div className="!py-6 w-full"></div>

If you're happy with the item, click the _**All good**_ button to complete your order.

<img src={guide17} className="max-w-screen-xs !my-12" />

If you're not happy with the item, click _**Something's wrong**_. See <LinkButton to="/guide/disputes-and-refunds" kind="tertiary" className="inline-flex">disputes and refunds</LinkButton> for more info.

<img src={guide18} className="max-w-screen-xs !my-12" />

If your order hasn't arrived after 7 days, click _**My order hasn't arrived yet**_ and we will be in touch.

<img src={guide19} className="max-w-screen-xs !my-12" />

If you don't notify us after 10 days, we will assume the item has arrived and will close the order.

> Once your order is completed, you cannot request a refund or dispute the order
