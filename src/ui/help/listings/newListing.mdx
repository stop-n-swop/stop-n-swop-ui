## Listing a game

Creating a new listing is easy and only takes a few steps:

1. **Find the game you want to sell**

   You can do that from this page, or head to the games page to fine tune your search. Each game's page has a "list this game" button.

2. **Enter your game's condition info**

   Tell us your game's region, condition, whether it's boxed, etc.

3. **Upload some photos**

   The uploads are specific to the game you're selling and the condition it's in. Only 1 photo is mandatory, but the more you upload, the better your listing's rating

4. **Create your listing!**

5. **Set up your paypal email**
   We use paypal to process payouts and withdrawals so you'll need to let us know what your preferred paypal email address is. It doesn't matter if you already have a paypal account or not.
