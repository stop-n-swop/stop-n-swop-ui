Once it has arrived, please check everything is okay. Is it in the advertised condition? Are all the bits and pieces there? Does it work?

Click the **Received** button to tell us whether you're happy with your purchase.

> If you're not happy with your item, choose the **Something's wrong** option and we'll get in touch.

> If you haven't received your item after 7 days, click the **I haven't received the item** button to let us know.

> If you don't respond after 10 days we'll assume the item arrived and you're just too busy enjoying it! Just keep in mind that after this point you can no longer request a refund.
